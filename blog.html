Most professional websites don't show what anthropologists like to call “messy humans”. This page leans into the messiness and the work in progress of my research, thinking, and writing that is not shown on my github or twitter account.

I am using this page to keep a running list of what I am reading, listening, and thinking. I won't list all papers, reports, or articles I have, for this you can have a look at my Zotero library:
zotero.org/christophbecker/library
The plan is to update new blogs/snippets haphazardly, share first drafts and half-baked thoughts.

The foci will lie on waste management and energy systems but might occasionally expand beyond to include other aspects including themes around science and technology studies.

Like a good geriatric millennial, I do not own a tv or a subscription to any video streaming services, but I am a fan of podcasts and mailinglists and will include my favorites here (see below).
